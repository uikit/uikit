Beyond Objective

is an online venture housing anime blog Deremoe and internet radio show Tech Rant.

Our name is our mission - To go beyond the borders, to go past the norm.

Ventures

Deremoe

Est. 2010

"We don't just share the news, we talk about it."

Deremoe first started as an anime blog made by Filipinos for the local audience. Now, it aims to be "a destination for opinion on the Philippine Otaku community." It serves anime, cosplay and related event updates and reviews.

deremoe.com

Tech Rant

Est. 2007

"We aim to be not just your ordinary tech show."

Tech Rant started as a podcast, discussing the tech side of things from a developer's perspective. Now, it's also an internet radio show and the technology side of the online anisong radio station Radyo Pirata .

techrant.beyondobjective.com
